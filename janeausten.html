Miss Elizabeth Bennet
Age: 20
Home: Hertfordshire

My greatest asset:
I believe my greatest asset is my wit, and the good-nature that I have learnt from being in a family of five young ladies! I am the greatest enjoyer of friendly banter, and I am unafraid to share my thoughts if I think them prevalent.

A quality I greatly admire:
I greatly admire someone who voices what he believes in and does not shy away from giving their opinion. However, I certainly will not stand by if these opinions offend; I believe a marriage must be equal, and each party should look up to the other as their superior.

One thing I hope to do together in the future:
I am a great reader; there is truly no enjoyment like it. I find afternoons employed in such a pursuit quite diverting, and I would happily spend a long walk discussing each of our novels.
Mr. Bingley
Age: 22
Home: Hertfordshire

My favourite pastime:
I adore making acquaintances and attending balls. There is nothing more diverting than forming friendships and entering new circles. I also quite enjoy hunting and shooting, and spending time out of doors.

One thing for which I am grateful:
I am quite grateful to have two very dear sisters, with whom I pass much time. They offer their advice remarkably freely, and though I do not always follow it, I appreciate their efforts to lead me in the best direction.

A quality I greatly admire:
I adore conversation, and I will get along swell with anyone with nearly so lively a disposition as I possess. My ideas often flow faster than I can write or speak them, so I greatly admire anyone who has the patience to listen to my ramblings.
Miss Harriet Smith
Home: Highbury

One thing for which I am grateful:
I am so very grateful to have made the acquaintance of a most lovely friend, Miss Woodhouse, whose very matchmaking service I am now using! I greatly admire Miss Woodhouse and thank her very kindly for all the grace she has shown me.

My favourite pastime:
I quite enjoy calling on my good friends in Highbury and conversing with them. I also adore collecting riddles and trying to solve them, although they often stump me!

One thing I hope to do together in the future:
I am quite fond of taking walks, and I should greatly enjoy doing so together! I recently had quite the scare when I encountered a group of gypsies in the woods, so I should quite like some sturdier company the next time I venture out.
